Nobant Viere

Character

About the Character Nobant Viere, or Unwilling follower in middle Vinecian/Luminaren is the protagonist of the inlore book that bears his name, which is based off the in world report by a Vinecian Senior Major in the newly formed Nisir Praeto
Significance of Nobant Viere Nobant Viere, despite being a fictional account, is actually based nearly exclusively on a singular report by one Senior Major of the 2nd Nisir Guard. What the book shows the reader is that the populace of Vinecia no longer wished to bear the burden of maintaing a future empire, and that they wanted a Vinecia for Vinecians with only Vinecian territory inside. This would radically change Vinecian foreign affairs and convince King -- along with the pleding of his advisors to not continue to expand the new Vinecian empire and to give his populace rest after 100 years of near constant warfare.
